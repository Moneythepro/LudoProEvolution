How to play (brief)

  1. Choose players (2–6) and tokens per player.
  2. Roll the dice. A 6 (if variant) lets a token exit home.
  3. Landing on opponent sends them home unless tile is safe.
  4. Bring all your tokens safely to home to win.

Variants: mustRoll6, bonus6, safeStars, exactHome.
